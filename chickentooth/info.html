About Chicken Tooth

Chicken Tooth is an app developed to allow the viewing of Rooster Teeth content on an iPad. This app does not claim ownership of any video or photo content on the app.

The goal of this app is not to compete with any Rooster Teeth brands, but to improve the experience for iPad users wanting to watch Rooster Teeth content.

If requested by Rooster Teeth this app will be taken down and used solely for personal use.

FAQ

  • Why do I need a FIRST account?

    Because I am streaming content from the Rooster Teeth website I have no way of serving their ads that are not baked into their website. Therefore to make sure I am not providing a bypass to Rooster Teeth ads I require FIRST membership.
